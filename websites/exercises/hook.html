Hook Head Lighthouse

A Beacon of History and Beauty in County Waterford

Climb the Tower and Discover History

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the top of Hook Head Lighthouse. Our guided tours offer fascinating insights into the lighthouse's 800-year history, from its monastic origins to its vital role in maritime safety today.

Ascend the spiral staircase, stopping at various levels to learn about the lives of the lightkeepers and the ingenious engineering behind this magnificent structure. The views from the balcony are simply breathtaking.

Explore the Visitor Centre and Café

After your climb, relax and refuel in our charming visitor centre and café. Browse our selection of local crafts, souvenirs, and educational exhibits that delve deeper into the rich heritage of Hook Head.

Enjoy a hot cup of coffee, a delicious scone, or a light lunch while taking in the panoramic sea views. It's the perfect spot to unwind and reflect on your visit.

Coastal Walks and Wildlife Spotting

The dramatic coastline surrounding Hook Head Lighthouse is a haven for nature lovers. Take a leisurely stroll along the designated coastal paths and experience the raw beauty of the Irish seascape.

Keep an eye out for diverse wildlife, including various seabirds, seals, and if you're lucky, even dolphins or whales. The fresh sea air and stunning vistas make for an invigorating experience.
